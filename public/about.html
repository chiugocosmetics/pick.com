About Clear & Calm Serum

Achieve a flawless complexion with the Clear & Calm Serum, expertly designed for sensitive skin.
This multifunctional serum combats pimples, dark spots, sunburn, and redness while promoting skin healing and brightening.

Key Benefits:

• Pimple Reduction: Clears and prevents breakouts.
• Dark Spot Correction: Fades dark spots and evens skin tone.
• Sunburn Relief: Soothes and repairs sun-damaged skin.
• Redness Reduction: Calms and reduces redness.
• Skin Brightening: Enhances natural radiance.
• Healing: Promotes overall skin health and repair.

Ingredients: Chamomile Hydrosol, Niacinamide (Vitamin B3), Licorice Root Extract, Centella Asiatica Extract, Vitamin E (Tocopherol), Panthenol (Pro-Vitamin B5), Hyaluronic Acid (1% solution), Chamomile Extract, Rosehip Seed Oil, Green Tea Extract, Salicylic Acid, Alpha Arbutin, Distilled Water, Preservative (e.g., Optiphen).

Directions for Use:

After cleansing, apply 3-5 drops of the Clear & Calm Serum to your fingertips or directly onto your face. Gently massage into your skin using upward and outward motions, avoiding the eye area. Allow the serum to absorb fully before applying moisturizer. Use twice daily, morning and evening, for optimal results. Reveal your skin’s true potential with Clear & Calm Serum. Experience the perfect blend of soothing care and effective results for a brighter, clearer, and calmer complexion.
